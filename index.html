My Journey in Entrepreneurship

Founder – Annam

2013 – Present

Established Annam to revolutionize the food industry by producing high-quality, sustainable batter products for diverse cuisines. With a strong focus on hygiene and consistency, Annam ensures that every product meets the highest industry standards. By integrating eco-friendly manufacturing practices, Annam is driving a shift toward sustainability in food production.

Founder – WakeUp Box

2024 – Present

Developed the concept for WakeUp Box, a daily essential food kit designed to provide families with all the necessary ingredients for a day's meals. This innovative idea aims to simplify meal planning while ensuring convenience and nutrition.

Co-Founder & CTO – Peppertech Systems Pvt Ltd

2021 – Present

Exploring the potential of customized 3D printing technology to revolutionize manufacturing and medical applications. This cutting-edge innovation aims to create customized, high-precision solutions for industries ranging from healthcare to industrial prototyping. By pushing the boundaries of material science and engineering, 3D bioprinting has the potential to redefine the future of production and medical advancements.
